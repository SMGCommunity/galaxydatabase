Class: Togezo

An enemy that carries a red spiky shell on its back. It can be defeated using most of the player's attacks, explosions, electricity, and some other objects such as Stretch Plants. However, touching its spikes will damage the player. Notably, it can be eaten and shot by Yoshi. It can be frozen by the Orange Luma.
