Config: StinkBugParent

A larger variant of a Mandibug that carries a child on its back. Likewise, it charges towards the player. It can be defeated by Ground Pounding its child and then its back. It can be frozen using the second player's pointer. It will spawn a Power Star when defeated, however, this requires a properly configured Scenario entry. In SMG2, this object won't appear initially unless it's forced to appear through cutscene actions.
