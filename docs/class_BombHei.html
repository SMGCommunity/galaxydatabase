Class: BombHei

A bomb or bomb-like enemy, depending on the object's name. Bomb creates a simple bomb that can be picked up and thrown whereas BombHei creates the actual enemy that will walk around and fuse if it sees Mario. It can be stunned and picked up after using a Spin Attack.
