Config: MarioFaceAfterGrandStarTakeOffDemoObj

Creates and registers several objects that appear in the cutscene where Starship Mario takes off to a new world after collecting a Grand Star. The individual models that it creates are DemoMarioFaceShipPlanet, Meister, WorldMapAccessPanel, MarioFaceShipRudder, and MiniWorldWarpPoint. The object needs to appear through a cutscene action.
