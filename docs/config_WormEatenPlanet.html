Config: WormEatenPlanet

A set of three giant apples with a green caterpillar connecting them. The caterpillar's movement is divided into three stages; first one being controlled by a cutscene and other two being controlled by switches.

Arguments

NameTypeGamesRequired?DescriptionValues
Point_arg0IntegerSMG1False

Point Behavior: Behavior of the caterpillar on this point.

  • -1: No special behavior
  • 0: The caterpillar enters an apple at this point
  • 1: The caterpillar comes to rest at this point, waiting for the next movement
  • 2: The caterpillar leaves an apple at this point

Switches

NameGamesRequired?Description
SW_ASMG1True

Starts the second movement of the caterpillar.

SW_BSMG1False

Starts the third movement of the caterpillar.
