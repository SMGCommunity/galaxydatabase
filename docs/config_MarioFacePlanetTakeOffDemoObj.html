Config: MarioFacePlanetTakeOffDemoObj

Creates and registers several objects that appear in the cutscene where Starship Mario takes off for the first time. The individual models that it creates are DemoMarioFaceShipPlanet, Meister, WorldMapAccessPanel, and MarioFaceShipRudder. It does nothing until SW_APPEAR gets activated.
