Class: SearchBeamer

A stationary robotic enemy that shoots lasers to attack the player. It cannot be defeated at all, but it will stop attacking for a short amount of time if licked by Yoshi. Also, it won't be able to shoot any lasers if the player stands on its head. It can be frozen by the Orange Luma.
