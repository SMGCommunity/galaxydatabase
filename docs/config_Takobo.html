Config: Takobo

An octopus enemy that can be defeated using most of the player's attacks. It can also be defeated by an enemy's explosion attack and Koopa Shells. It moves a set distance. Once the player gets close, it will headbutt the player (SMG1) or shoot a rock at the player (SMG2). It drops a Coin when stomped or Ground Pounded and it drops Star Bits when damaged through other means.
