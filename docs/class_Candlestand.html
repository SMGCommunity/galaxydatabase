Class: Candlestand

A torch with a flame that can burn the player upon contact. Usually, the flame can be put out using a Spin Attack to make an item appear, however, the flame of CandlestandIceVolcano cannot be put out. Said variant may also appear without a flame if it uses a SW_A. Instead, the player can use Fire Balls to lit the fire and activate the switch.
