Config: ClipFieldSwitch

A controller that toggles its SW_A and SW_B switches depending on whether the player is inside a ClipArea or not.

Switches

NameGamesRequired?Description
SW_ABothFalse

Gets activated if the player is inside a ClipArea and gets deactivated when the player is outside a ClipArea.

SW_BBothFalse

Gets deactivated if the player is inside a ClipArea and gets activated when the player is outside a ClipArea.
