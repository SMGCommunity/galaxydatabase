Class: SmallStone

A circle of eight smaller objects that can be released from the ground by using a Spin Attack while inside the circle. Each chunk will spawn a Star Bit when released. The chunks attach to the nearest collision below them. If no collision is available, the individual plant may be placed at the origin of the galaxy.
