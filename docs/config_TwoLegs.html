Config: TwoLegs

A robotic boss that can stomp the player. Furthermore, it may launch Diggas (TwoLegsBullet) to attack the player. The Diggas have to be placed and linked to it. After hitting the glass cage three times with the Spin Drill, it will be defeated which makes the caged Power Star appear. However, this requires a properly configured Scenario entry. If SW_A is not used, the boss battle will start immediately.
