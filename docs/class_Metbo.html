Class: Metbo

A Goomba wearing a metal helmet. Therefore, they have to be stunned with a Spin Attack first before the player can stomp its weak spot. However, they can be defeated using the Rainbow power-up, Yoshi's projectiles or by having Yoshi eat them. It drops a Coin or three Star Bits depending on how it was defeated.
