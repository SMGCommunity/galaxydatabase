Class: Butterfly

A decorative butterfly. It can spawn a Star Bit when touched with the pointer. If the player is sleeping near a butterfly, it will eventually land on their nose.

Obj_arg0IntegerBothFalse

Color: Sets the BRK color frame.

  • -1: Orange with purple outline
  • 1: Dark red with blue outline
  • 2: Red with navy blue outline
  • 3: Bright green with yellow outline
