Class: Dodoryu

A large mole boss. In SMG1, he chases a Star Bunny before the actual battle begins. He attacks the player upon contact. The player needs to damage him three times to defeat him. It also creates trails of dirt when it moves around. He is usually underground but can be stunned using a Ground Pound when his head is above the ground. This gives the player a chance to attack him. In SMG1, it always spawns a Power Star and uses text for the Star Bunny it chases.
