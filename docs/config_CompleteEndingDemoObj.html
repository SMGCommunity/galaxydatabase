Config: CompleteEndingDemoObj

Creates and registers several objects that appear during the ending cutscene in the Comet Observatory's library. The individual models that it creates are RosettaReading, RosettaChair, TicoBaby and MarioCap or LuigiCap. The object needs to appear through a cutscene action.
