Config: Tongari

An undefeatable enemy that carries a white cone-shaped shell with spikes on its back. It moves on a set path. Tongari turns around while moving and creates bubble particles when hit; Tongari2D always faces sideways and creates smoke clouds when hit. It can be frozen by the Orange Luma.
