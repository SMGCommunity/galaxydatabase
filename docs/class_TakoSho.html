Class: TakoSho

An Octoomba that can be defeated using most of the player's attacks. It can also be defeated by an enemy's explosion attack and Koopa Shells. It moves a set distance. Once the player gets close, it will shoot two rocks in the player's direction, one at a time, to damage them. It drops a Coin when stomped or Ground Pounded and it drops Star Bits when damaged through other means. It can be frozen by the Orange Luma.
