Config: Nyoropon

A tube worm-like enemy that opens its eyes and tries to block the player's movement when approached. When the player touches it, they'll be damaged. It can be defeated with a Koopa Shell or by the light shone by the shell itself. Furthermore, the player can use Spin Attacks or Star Bits to defeat it. This causes it to drop three Star Bits. It can be frozen by the Orange Luma.
