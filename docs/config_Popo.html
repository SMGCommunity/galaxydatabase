Config: Popo

A pink enemy that tries to cling onto Yoshi, but runs away from the player under normal circumstances. It can be defeated using Spin Attacks, Ground Pounds or by stomping on top of it. Furthermore, it can be shaken off Yoshi, which defeats it as well.
