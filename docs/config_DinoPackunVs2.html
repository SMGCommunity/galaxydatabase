Config: DinoPackunVs2

A large flaming Piranha Plant boss. Before the battle, it remains inside its shell. In order to defeat it, the player has to fling its tail ball with a Spin Attack four times. It always damages the player upon contact. However, the large tail ball does not hurt the player unless it's burning. It will sporadically shoot fireballs during the last phase and create fiery trails that burn the player upon contact. It will always spawn a Power Star when defeated, which requires a properly configured Scenario entry.
