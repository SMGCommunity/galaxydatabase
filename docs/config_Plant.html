Config: Plant

A beanstalk-like vine that the player can swing along by shaking the Wiimote. Once the player reaches the end of the vine, it will cause them to jump off. The launch speed and angle are configurable.

Arguments

NameTypeGamesRequired?DescriptionValues
Obj_arg0FloatBothFalse

Launch Speed: How fast the player gets flinged off after reaching the end of the plant's path. Default is 30.

    Obj_arg1FloatBothFalse

    Launch Angle: At what angle the player gets launched off after reaching the end of the plant's path. Default is 0.

      Obj_arg2BooleanBothFalse

      Play Appearance Camera?: If set, it will play a short event camera cutscene when its tuber gets popped.
