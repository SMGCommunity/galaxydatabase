Config: RollingWaterCreator

Creates rolling water bubbles that trap the player upon contact. The player can free themself using attacks. Furthermore, it can be frozen and popped by the Orange Luma. The bubbles can move on a set path or roll around with the help of gravity. Bubbles that don't move on a path will pop once they enter a RollingWaterBreakArea.
