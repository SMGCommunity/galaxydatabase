Class: KoopaJrCastle

A castle tank boss that is controlled by Bowser Jr. It features rotating three rotating Fire Bars, vents that suck in cloud platforms and a large glass capsule. The player has to Ground Pound the glass three times to defeat the boss. After hitting it twice, the lower portion will emerge from the ground, allowing it to move around the arena. It always spawns a Power Star when defeated.
