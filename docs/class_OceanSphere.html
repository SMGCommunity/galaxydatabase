Class: OceanSphere

A sphere of water that essentially functions as a visible WaterArea. If the stage's name is TearDropGalaxy, it becomes translucent and uses a lighter shade of blue; if the stage's name is SkullSharkGalaxy, it retains the dark blue color but becomes translucent as well. When it appears through a cutscene, the water will scale up first.
