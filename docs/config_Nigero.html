Config: Nigero

A frog-like enemy that jumps and burrows into the ground. If it does so, it will reappear out of a random spot again. They will try to pick up an item if they find one and run away with it. It can pick up Coins, Purple Coins, Silver Stars, Star Chips and Blue Chips. They can be defeated by having Yoshi eat them or by stomping on top of them. However, they will reappear again after a few seconds.
