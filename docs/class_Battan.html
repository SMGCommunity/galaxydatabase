Class: Battan

A stone block enemy that moves on a fixed path. Once it sees Mario, it will move towards him to fall down on him. Once it lies on the ground, the weak point on its back can be Ground Pounded. Can be frozen by the Orange Luma. It will spawn 7 Star Bits if defeated.
